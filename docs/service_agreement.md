# OpenClaw Service Agreement

This Service Agreement ("Agreement") governs the provision of OpenClaw services to the Customer. By engaging with OpenClaw, the Customer agrees to the following terms.

## 1. Service Definitions & Scope

OpenClaw services are primarily delivered as **automated bot services**. The scope of these automated services includes:
- **Self-Host Setup:** Guidance and automated scripts for provisioning OpenClaw on Customer-owned hardware.
- **Self-Host Support:** Automated troubleshooting and technical documentation retrieval.
- **Concierge/Custom Assistance:** Specialized AI agent workflows as currently offered within the OpenClaw ecosystem.

OpenClaw is a self-hosted software solution; it is not a hosted Software-as-a-Service (SaaS) product. The Customer maintains full ownership and responsibility for their hosting environment.

## 2. Pricing and Billing Terms

- **Automated Service Layer:** All standard listed pricing and subscription fees apply strictly to the **automated bot service layer**. 
- **Human-Session Surcharge:** If the Customer requests or requires a **human session**, live consultation, or direct manual operator involvement that falls outside the automated service flow, such work shall be billed as a separate surcharge.

## 3. Human-Session Surcharge Terms

- **Rate:** Human sessions are billed at a flat rate of **.00 per hour**.
- **Billing Increment:** Sessions are billed in minimum increments of 30 minutes.
- **Trigger:** A human-session surcharge is triggered upon the Customer's explicit request for a live operator or when the complexity of a support task requires manual intervention beyond the AI's capabilities, subject to prior Customer approval.

## 4. Customer Responsibilities for Self-Hosted Environments

- **Infrastructure:** The Customer is solely responsible for the procurement, maintenance, and security of the hardware (e.g., Mac Mini, VPS, or On-prem server) used to run OpenClaw.
- **Connectivity:** The Customer must ensure stable network connectivity and appropriate remote access (e.g., Tailscale or SSH) for support services to function.
- **Backups:** The Customer is responsible for maintaining regular backups of their OpenClaw workspace and configuration.

## 5. Support Limitations & Scope Boundaries

- Support is limited to the functional operation of the OpenClaw software and its native agents.
- Out-of-scope items include: third-party hardware repair, OS-level security hardening beyond OpenClaw's requirements, and custom software development not explicitly covered in a separate Statement of Work.

## 6. Disclaimer and Liability

- **As-Is Basis:** OpenClaw is provided on an "as-is" and "as-available" basis.
- **Limitation of Liability:** In no event shall OpenClaw or its operators be liable for any indirect, incidental, or consequential damages arising out of the use or inability to use the self-hosted software. 
- **Lightweight Agreement:** This document serves as a lightweight service agreement appropriate for the OpenClaw self-host customer success model.
